### **Title:**

Promote Database Development Objects to QA Environment

---

### **Description:**

Promote all finalized database development objects related to the Claims Engine project from the Development environment to the QA environment. This includes tables, views, procedures, streams, tasks, and supporting configuration tables such as `CLAIMS_SUPPLEMENTAL_PROPERTIES`.

---

### **Acceptance Criteria:**

* All database objects are reviewed and approved for promotion.
* Verified DDL and DML scripts are executed in QA successfully.
* QA instance reflects the latest schema structure and reference data.
* Functional dependencies (e.g., procedures, streams, tasks) are validated post-deployment.
* Confirmation from QA team that the environment is ready for integration testing.
